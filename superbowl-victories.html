Super Bowl Victories

SuperBowl XVI was between the San Francisco 49ers and the Cincinnati Bengals. The game ended with the score of 26-21. Joe Montana was the SuperBowl MVP.

In SuperBowl XIX, we faced Dan Marino and the Miami Dolphins. The game ended 38 - 16. Joe Montana threw for 331 Yards and 3 touchdowns, allowing him to win his second SuperBowl MVP.

SuperBowl XXIII was a rematch of SuperBowl XVI. The game ended 20-16 but was not decided until the final seconds of the game. SuperBowl MVP was awarded to Jerry Rice, who is arguably one of the best offensive players the NFL has ever seen.

SuperBowl XXIV was a complete blow out from the start against the Broncos. The 49ers ended up winning 55-10. This is the 4th SuperBowl for the 49ers and the first time they have won the SuperBowl back to back. Joe Montana was awarded the SuperBowl MVP once again.

The final SuperBowl the 49ers won was in 1995 in SuperBowl XXIV against the Chargers. Steve Young thew for 325 Yards and six touchdowns, most in SuperBowl history. Due to his legendary performance, he was able to add a SuperBowl MVP to his trophy cabinet.
