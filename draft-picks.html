New Faces on the Block

Ji'Ayir Brown from Penn State who played safety for the previous two years will be an amazing fit for our squad. With Jimmmy Ward leaving this offseason to the Texans, he will lots of opportunities to show what he is capable of.

Dee Winters is listed at 5'11, 227 pounds with a sense of the ball. Recording 246 tackles, 12.5 sacks, and three interceptions during his tenure at TCU. Playing between Greenlaw and Warner, he might develope into the third LB to complete this trio.

The 49ers are known for having a D-line with interchangeable players who are young explosive and dangers within our scheme. With Beal Jr. coming in at 6'3, 247 pounds and wingspan of 83 inches, he is a player to lookout for in this upcoming season.
